Methodology Snapshot

  • Precursor momentum: blend of Golden Globes, Critics Choice, SAG, BAFTA, PGA/DGA/WGA relevance.
  • Historical fit: genre/branch affinity, distributor track record, release corridor timing, and campaign capacity.
  • Buzz: festival reaction, critic sentiment, and social/industry chatter.
  • Nomination odds: calibrated by a logistic transform and normalized within category.
  • Winner odds: nomination odds multiplied by conversion factors learned from recent Academy cycles.
